BARANGAY PROFILE

Salcedo is a barangay in the municipality of San Manuel. Its population as determined by the 2021 Census was 3,157. This represented 9.28% of the total population of San Manuel. The town of San Manuel is a typically rural community located at the northernmost horn of the Province of Tarlac.

  • Number of Population = 3,157
  • Number of Household = 624
  • Number of Voters = 1,324

VISION

A strong and progressive Barangay of Salcedo where socio-economic activities are anchored on the empowerment and discipline of the people, impassioned for peace; Godliness, good like and education; alongside with enhancement of commercial, agricultural and industrial developments; while nurturing a healthy environment; and, utilizing judiciously its hard-earned resources.

MISSION

To transform Salcedo into a future-ready barangay through sustained social, economic and environmental programs that shall promote and sustain moral, health, education and peace; alongside with modernizing agriculture and creating commercial activities that will bring real progress and quality life of the people.
